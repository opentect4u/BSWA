BOSEC Welfare Association

Established on 29th Day of May 2010 and Registered under Socity ACT

Membership of the Association

The members of BOSEC WELFAE TRUST at their first meeting have constituted and form an association of the Beneficiaries under the name of BOKARO STEEL PEOPLE WELFARE ASSOCIATION. Members of the said Association is the beneficiaries of the Trust. The Committee of the association is admit and enroll the members as provided herein after. The Association is registered with the Register of Societies under the Societies Registration act and comply with all formalities of the said act.

Eligibility of Memberships

  1. Who is a present and past employees of SAIL, BOKARO STEEL PLANT
  2. Who resides in the area of operation of the Association
  3. Who has a reputable character and is in sound mind and never convicted in court of law
  4. Who agrees to abide by the constitution and the by laws of the Association and supports its aims and objectives.
  5. In case of death of member, the spouse is entitled to enjoy the membership till his/her death subject to other conditions and will enjoy the same type of membership .

Categories of Memberships.

  1. Founder Members: These members consist of those that were signatories in the application made before the Registering Authority  and will pay initially Rs.10, 000/- (Rupees Ten thousand) only to create initial Association Fund.
  2. Life Members: A person who pays Rs. 40,000/- (Rupees Forty thousands only), in lump sum to the Association,  shall be invited by the Committee  to become a life member of the Assocation . .(In case of members/ nominal members of Bokaro Steel Employees’(Calcutta) Co operative Credit Society Limited, the donation  amount will be Rs. 2000/-instead of Rs.40000/) -However, A life member has to Pay Rs. 100/- per year for renewal of yearly membership.
  3. General Members:  A person, whose application for membership is approved by the Committee , shall become ordinary member of the Association on payment of the annual membership fees of Rs.240/- (Rupees two hundred foty Only).
  4. Associate Members : All the dependent family members have to enroll their name as Associate Members and have to pay annual membership fees of Rs.250/-(Rupees five hundreds ) only
  5.  Affiliate Members: An agency registered under the Indian Association Act /Societies Registration Act subscribing to the aims and objectives of the Assocation , subject to the approval of the Committee , can become its associate member on payment of annual membership fee of Rs.1200 (Rupees Twelve Hundreds Only).
  6. Honorary Member: A person who has rendered eminent service to the cause of Social Indian Association Act or Co-operates actively with the Association   may be nominated as Honorary Member by the Committee of the Association  He shall not be required to pay any membership fee. Condition A (i) & (ii), of the Article (6), shall not apply to this category of membership.
  7. Co-Opted Members: Committee of the Association  can appoint co-opted member(s) for special purpose(s)

Procedure for admission to various categories of Membership

  1. Founder Members: As Founder Members of the organization, these members are automatically considered to be holders of the Membership in this category. Conditions of payment of   any type of Fees shall not apply on them
  2. Life Members: Persons having qualification, as laid down at Article 1  and desirous to become a Life Member or Ordinary Member shall apply on prescribed form of the Association to the Committee  along with an admission /application fee of Rs.50/-, which shall have the power to accept or reject any such application. A person shall become life member or ordinary member only after payment of the prescribed fee.
  3. General Members: Persons having qualifications, as laid down above  shall apply on a prescribed form of the Association  for the said Membership along with an admission /application fee of Rs.50/-. The Committee shall have the right to accept or reject any such application. A person shall become General Member only after payment of the prescribed fee
  4. Associate Members / Persons having qualifications, as laid down in, Article 1 shall apply on a prescribed form of the Association  for the said Membership along with an admission /application fee of Rs.50/-. The Committee  shall have the right to accept or reject any such application. A person shall become Associate Member only after payment of the prescribed fee.
  5. Affiliated Members: Any agency who fulfils the conditions laid down under Article 1 can apply to the Committee of the Association   to become Associated or Affiliated Member, which shall have the power to accept or reject any such application. The agency shall become Associate Member or Affiliate Member only after payment of prescribed fee.
